Is Brave Browser the Answer?

-NoobScience

Say you are looking for a alternative to Chrome, ignoring the fact why. Maybe you want to be more private or maybe you want a less ram hungry browser or maybe you are sick of ads, but still want the chromium experince and security, regardless, you have many options and few of them are very popular like Chromium, Ungoogled-Chromium and the Iridium Browser, but most of them don't have regular updates and are just a kind of hobby projects. Enter Brave Browser, it come with built in Ad-Block, javascript control including many othher features. Brave Browser is actually the child product of the creator of javascript, Brendan Eich who is also a co-founder of the Mozilla foundation.
